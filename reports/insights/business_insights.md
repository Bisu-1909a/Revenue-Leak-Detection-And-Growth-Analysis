# Business Insights

## 1. Total Revenue

### Observation
The total revenue generated by the business is **₹33,288,790.78** after applying all discounts.

### Business Insight
The company has generated strong overall sales revenue. This KPI provides management with a clear understanding of the business's financial performance and serves as the baseline for comparing future sales growth.

---

## 2. Top Revenue Products

### Observation
The top five revenue-generating products are Laptop Pro, Smart TV, Wireless Earbuds, Running Shoes, and Office Chair.

### Business Insight
These products are the company's strongest revenue drivers. Management should ensure adequate inventory, increase promotional efforts, and consider bundling or upselling these products to maximize profitability.

---

## 3. Revenue by Category

### Observation
Revenue was calculated for each product category and ranked from highest to lowest.

### Business Insight
The highest-performing categories contribute the largest share of total revenue. Marketing campaigns and inventory planning should focus on these categories while lower-performing categories should be reviewed for pricing, product assortment, or promotional improvements.

---

## 4. Revenue by Region

### Observation
The East region generated the highest revenue, while the North region generated the lowest revenue.

### Business Insight
Regions with higher revenue indicate strong customer demand and business performance. Lower-performing regions may require additional marketing campaigns, improved logistics, or region-specific promotional strategies to increase sales.

---

## 5. Customer Segment Analysis

### Observation
Different customer segments contribute different amounts of revenue.

### Business Insight
The highest-value customer segment represents the company's most profitable customer base. Customer retention programs, loyalty rewards, and personalized marketing campaigns should focus on these customers to increase long-term revenue.

---

## 6. Discount Analysis

### Observation
Revenue and sales volume were analyzed across different discount percentages.

### Business Insight
Discounts influence customer purchasing behavior. Higher discounts do not always generate proportionally higher revenue. The company should optimize its discount strategy to balance increased sales volume with overall profitability.

---

## 7. Sales Channel Analysis

### Observation
Revenue and order volume were compared across Website, Mobile App, and Marketplace sales channels.

### Business Insight
The highest-performing sales channel contributes the largest portion of total revenue. Management should continue investing in this channel while improving lower-performing channels to provide a balanced customer experience.

---

## 8. Delivery Performance

### Observation
Courier performance and delivery status were analyzed to identify delayed deliveries.

### Business Insight
Delivery performance directly affects customer satisfaction. Couriers with higher delay rates should be reviewed to improve logistics efficiency, reduce delivery times, and enhance customer experience.

---

## 9. Monthly Revenue Trend

### Observation
Revenue was analyzed month by month to identify sales trends throughout the two-year period.

### Business Insight
Monthly trend analysis helps identify seasonal demand patterns and business growth. Months with higher revenue indicate peak sales periods, allowing management to prepare inventory, staffing, and marketing campaigns in advance.
