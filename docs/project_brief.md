# Business Case

ABC Mart is an e-commerce company that sells products across multiple regions. Although total sales have increased over the past year, overall profitability has remained almost unchanged.

Management suspects that hidden revenue leaks are reducing profit. Possible causes include excessive discounts, low-margin products, delayed deliveries, cancelled orders, returned orders, and declining customer activity.

The Business Analytics team has been assigned to investigate these issues. The objective is to identify where revenue is leaking, estimate the financial impact, and recommend practical business actions to improve profitability and support future growth.
